---

## Chosen Problem Statement

Many startup founders struggle to create professional startup pitches, MVP plans, and investor-ready documentation quickly. They often lack business strategy guidance, structured planning, and presentation skills.

The AI Startup Pitch Assistant helps founders:
- Organize startup ideas
- Generate pitch deck content
- Plan MVP features
- Analyze competitors
- Export investor-ready summaries

---

## MVP Features

### 1. Startup Idea Generator
Users enter startup concepts and receive AI-generated summaries.

### 2. Pitch Deck Content Builder
Generate:
- Problem statement
- Solution
- Target audience
- Revenue model

### 3. Competitor Analysis
AI suggests:
- Similar startups
- Market gaps
- Competitive advantages

### 4. MVP Roadmap Planner
Generate:
- Prioritized feature lists
- Development phases
- MVP recommendations

### 5. PDF Export
Export startup summaries into shareable PDF reports.
